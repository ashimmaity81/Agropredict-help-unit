# ❓ FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

Find answers to common questions about AgroPredict and how it helps farmers make smarter agricultural decisions.

---

## 1. What is AgroPredict?
AgroPredict is an AI-powered web application that helps farmers select the most suitable crops based on soil and environmental conditions.

---

## 2. How does AgroPredict work?
It analyzes user inputs like soil nutrients (N, P, K), temperature, humidity, pH, and rainfall using a machine learning model to recommend the best crop.

---

## 3. Is AgroPredict free to use?
Yes, AgroPredict is designed as a free platform for farmers and students to access intelligent crop recommendations.

---

## 4. What inputs are required for crop prediction?
You need to provide:
- Nitrogen (N)
- Phosphorus (P)
- Potassium (K)
- Temperature
- Humidity
- pH level
- Rainfall

---

## 5. How accurate are the predictions?
The predictions are based on trained machine learning models and datasets, offering high accuracy when correct inputs are provided.

---

## 6. Can beginners use AgroPredict?
Yes, the platform is designed with a simple and user-friendly interface suitable for beginners.

---

## 7. Does AgroPredict support multiple languages?
Yes, it includes multilingual support so farmers from different regions can use it easily.

---

## 8. Is internet connection required?
Yes, since AgroPredict is a web-based application, an internet connection is required.

---

## 9. Can I access AgroPredict on mobile devices?
Yes, the platform is fully responsive and works on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.

---

## 10. Does AgroPredict store my data?
Yes, user inputs and prediction history are stored securely to help track previous recommendations.

---

## 11. What technology is used in AgroPredict?
- Frontend: React + Vite + Tailwind CSS  
- Backend: FastAPI  
- Database: MongoDB  
- Machine Learning: scikit-learn  

---

## 12. What is N, P, and K in farming?
N (Nitrogen), P (Phosphorus), and K (Potassium) are essential nutrients required for plant growth and productivity.

---

## 13. Can AgroPredict help increase crop yield?
Yes, by suggesting suitable crops based on data, it helps farmers make better decisions that can improve yield.

---

## 14. Is my personal data सुरक्षित?
Yes, AgroPredict follows secure authentication and data storage practices to protect user information.

---

## 15. Can I view my previous predictions?
Yes, AgroPredict stores history so you can review past crop recommendations anytime.

---

## 16. Does AgroPredict provide weather data?
It can integrate weather-based inputs, but predictions depend on the values entered by the user.

---

## 17. What if I enter incorrect data?
Incorrect inputs may lead to inaccurate predictions, so it's important to provide correct values.

---

## 18. Can AgroPredict detect crop diseases?
Currently, it focuses on crop recommendation, but future versions may include disease detection features.

---

## 19. Who can use AgroPredict?
Farmers, students, researchers, and anyone interested in agriculture and smart farming can use it.

---

## 20. How can I contact support?
You can use the Support section or contact form available on the AgroPredict website for assistance.
